Lasanga Recipe

Description

This is a sick very cool, very yummy lasanga. I promise it is not actually goulash.

Ingredients


  • 1 pound lean groudn turkey
  • 1(14 ounce) can stewed, diced tomatos
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 cup tomato sauce
  • 2 teaspoons white sugar
  • 1/2 teaspoon dried basil
  • 1 (16 ounce) package bow tie pasta

Steps

  1. In a large skillet over medium hear, cook the turkey until browned
  2. Stir in the stewed tomatoes, garlic, tomato sauce, sugar and basil, and then simmer for about 20 minutes./li>
  3. Bring a large pot of lightly salted water to a boil. Add pasta and cook for 8 to 10 minutes or until al dente, and then drain. Combine the pasta and turkey mixture; toss and serve.
